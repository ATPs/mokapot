### mokapot

- **mokapot** is a tool for the analysis of protein identification data from mass spectrometry experiments. It is a Python-based tool that can be used to analyze data from various types of mass spectrometry experiments, including tandem mass spectrometry (MS/MS) and ion mobility mass spectrometry (IM-MS).
